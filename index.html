Welcome to the instruction page


  • The aim of the game is to get the highest score in 10 seconds
  • To do this, you need to click on as many balls as you can until the time runs out. Your final score is your hits minus your misses multiplied by your remaining time
  • Each ball you click will add one to your hits, but be careful, everytime you miss...
  • The game ends when your time is up, or you have clicked all the balls
